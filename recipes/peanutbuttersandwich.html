Peanut Butter and Banana Sandwich

Sandwich

Whole wheat bread with peanut butter and banana slices

Ingredients

  • Whole wheat bread
  • Banana
  • Peanut butter

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter over one slice of bread
  2. Cut banana into slices and place evenly over slice of bread with peanut butter
  3. Place this slice and other together with banana and peanut butter in between
