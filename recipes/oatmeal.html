Vegan Oatmeal

Vegan Protein Oatmeal with berries and peanut butter

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Soy Milk
  • Plant Protein Powder
  • Mixed Frozen Berries
  • Creatine
  • Peanut Butter
  • Cranberry Raisins

Steps

  1. Add 80g of oats to bowl
  2. Pour 1 cup of soy milk over oats
  3. Microwave on high for 3 minutes
  4. Add frozen berries and mix
  5. Add peanut butter, creatine, and cranberry raisins and mix thorougly
